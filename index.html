Welcome to Marigold Ballon Safaris

Marigold Balloon safaris offers balloon safaris over the iconic Golden Delta, a vast inland delta which floods during the winter season, supplying wildlife with a much-needed water supply, and creating one of Asia's greatest concentrations of wildlife. Marigold Ballon safaris operates in the north western part of the Golden city, over an area flooded with water and winding channels, dotted with islands, and large expanses of woodland forests. A rich diversity of wildlife can be seen from the balloon, as this age-old adventure explores the Golden Delta in a completely unique way, offering a 360-degree panoramic view from tree top level to a thousand feet in the air.
